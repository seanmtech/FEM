Meet the Founders.

In 2020, a team of creatives thought up the idea of the Female Empowerment Mission. Meet the founders that set out to diversify the Great Lakes startup environment.

Christina Rivas

Business Analyst

Christina Rivas is a senior pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering at UW-Madison. She is the President of the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers and is passionate about increasing the diversity within STEM.

Isaac Wells-Cage

Market Strategist

Isaac Wells-Cage is an upcoming sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. He plans to pursue a degree in Mechanical Engineering, with the goal of working in the renewable energy sector or in mechatronics. Isaac is passionate about spreading access to information and resources to underrepresented communities.

Megan Huth

Brand & Graphic Designer

Megan Huth is a junior pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Graphic Design at St. Norbert College. She is the Marketing and Communication Coordinator for the SNC career center as well as a collegiate athlete on the women’s soccer team.
